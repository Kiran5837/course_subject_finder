Course Subjects

The course subjects section defines what applicants studied in that specific department. As per the CAS rule, all the course subjects should match the course title or department.

Problem

Most of the course subjects should match the course title, but sometimes verifiers may enter incorrect course subjects that may lead to escalation.

Solution

If the portal can automatically identify and update the course subjects from the course title, this will reduce course subject escalations. Click here to search for courses.
